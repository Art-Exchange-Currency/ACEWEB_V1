Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Art Currency Exchange (ACE)?

ACE is a luxury platform where art meets investment. We host exclusive auctions where users can bid on premium artworks, invest in fractional shares, and receive unique NFTs to ensure digital ownership and authenticity.

Who can participate in auctions?

Anyone aged 18 or above can participate after creating an account and completing the necessary verification process.

Bidding & Buying

How does the bidding process work?

Bidders place offers during the auction window. The highest bid at the end of the auction wins. Verified users may get a second-chance opportunity in case of payment failure by the highest bidder.

Can I buy artwork without bidding?

Yes! Select artworks have a 'Buy Now' option. You can purchase these instantly without participating in the auction.

NFTs & Ownership

What is the significance of NFTs on ACE?

Every artwork purchased on ACE comes with an exclusive NFT. This NFT serves as proof of ownership, provides digital protection against forgery, and increases visibility for your artwork within the Web3 space.

How is my NFT stored?

You can connect your wallet to ACE to store your NFTs securely. Compatible wallets include MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and more.
